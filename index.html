1) CHAMELEON

Interesting facts about chameleons :

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are of the iguana suborder
  • Chameleons have transparent skin
  • Changing skin color is an important form of communication among the chameleons
  • Beneath the special skin is pigment ,the substance that gives color to plants and animals

    Interesting facts about cicadas

  • Cicadas are physically distinguished by their stout bodies , large head ,clear membraned wings and large compound eyes
  • Cicadas usually live for 4 - 5 years
  • Cicadas produce an unusually loud sound
